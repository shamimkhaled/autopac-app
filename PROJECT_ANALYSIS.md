## 1. Executive Summary

Auto Pac is a B2B machinery supplier based in Dhaka, Bangladesh (128/3 Kawran Bazar). The website functions as an **inquiry-generation catalog** (no cart/checkout), targeting factory owners, SME entrepreneurs, and business setup seekers. The primary goal is lead capture via "Request a Quote" and "Inquire on WhatsApp" CTAs.

## 7. UI Structure & Content Hierarchy

### 7.1 Sitemap
```
/ (Home)
├── /products (Catalog – Grid view)
│   ├── /products?category=food-beverage
│   ├── /products?category=health-pharma
│   ├── /products?category=cosmetics-toiletries
│   ├── /products?category=industrial
│   └── /products/[slug] (Product Detail)
├── /about
├── /contact
└── /support (Future: QR code landing – spare parts, technician request)
```

### 7.2 Home Page Structure
1. **Hero Slider** – 3 slides (slider1, 2, 3), headlines from images
2. **Quick Category Links** – 4 cards: Food, Pharma, Cosmetics, Industrial
3. **Trusted By** – Brand logos carousel
4. **Featured Machines** – 6–8 products, grid
5. **CTA Strip** – “Request a Quote” / “Inquire on WhatsApp”

### 7.3 Product Catalog Page
- Sidebar: Category filter (collapsible on mobile)
- Grid view (responsive: 1 col mobile, 2–3 tablet, 4 desktop)
- Product card: Image, name, short description, “What it packs” tags (first 3–4), CTA

### 7.4 Product Detail Page
1. **Image Gallery** – High-res, zoom, thumbnails
2. **Specification Table** – Key-value pairs (Voltage, Speed, Weight, Packing Range)
3. **“What Can This Machine Pack?”** – Tags/icons from packable list
4. **Video Embed** – YouTube/Vimeo iframe
5. **Sticky CTA (Mobile)** – “Request Quote” | “Inquire on WhatsApp”

### 7.5 About Page
- Experience in industry
- Technical support capabilities
- Team/company story (if available)

### 7.6 Contact Page
- Google Maps embed
- WhatsApp API link (pre-filled message)
- Lead form: Name, Phone, Email, Company, Product Interest, Message
- Address, Phone, Email display

## 8. Bilingual Support Architecture

- **Toggle:** EN | বাংলা in header
- **Storage:** All user-facing text in `_en` and `_bn` columns
- **URL:** Option A: Same URL, content switches via JS/state. Option B: `/bn/products` prefix
- **RTL:** Not required (Bengali uses LTR)
- **Font:** Bengali-friendly (e.g., Noto Sans Bengali, SolaimanLipi)
