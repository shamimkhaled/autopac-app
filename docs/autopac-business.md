# Auto Pac Business Profile

## Business Summary
**Auto Pac** is a premier importer and local manufacturer of world-class food processing and packing machineries in Bangladesh. They provide total solutions for various industries including food, cosmetics, herbal (Ayurvedic/Unani), and chemicals.

**Tagline:** Auto Pac machineries without hand touch automatically packing (অটো প্যাক মেশিনে, হাতের ছোঁয়া ছাড়াই স্বয়ংক্রিয় ভাবে প্যাক করে)
**Core Value:** Quality and service without compromise.

## Services & Product Categories
Auto Pac offers a wide range of machineries:
1.  **Agro Products:** Rice, Atta, Maida, Dal, Puffed Rice, Sugar, Salt processing and packing.
2.  **Beverage & Liquid:** Water treatment plants (U-V & RO), juice, carbonated drinks, beverage filling lines.
3.  **Snacks & Confectionery:** Chanachur, chips, dal, motor baja plants, biscuits (soft/hard), candy processing lines.
4.  **Dairy & Oil:** Dairy milk, chocolate bar, mango bar plants, mustard and soyabean oil processing.
5.  **Health & Beauty:** Cosmetic, herbal, ayurvedic, and Unani product processing and packing.
6.  **Industrial Solutions:** Frozen foods vacuum packing, bottle blow molding, fish/poultry feed processing, mosquito coil machineries.
7.  **Support:** Spare parts and professional servicing.

## Value Proposition
- **Automated Solutions:** Reducing human touch for hygiene and efficiency.
- **Versatility:** Catering to multiple industries from food to chemicals.
- **Quality Assurance:** "World-class" machineries with dedicated service.
- **Experience:** Serving major clients like ACI, Unilever, Pran, and Bashundhara.
